The sales enablement director runs onboarding, certification, and continuous learning for a revenue org. Ramp time is their headline metric; certification-pass rate and rep skill verification are right behind. They care about integrations with the CRM and the call-intelligence stack, and they want learning content to update when the product does, not on a training-calendar delay.
