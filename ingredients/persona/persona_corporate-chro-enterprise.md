The enterprise CHRO owns people strategy across a 5,000+ employee organization. Learning is one of their portfolios, not their whole job. They care about business outcomes attached to learning — hiring ramp, retention, compliance posture, skills inventory defensibility in an M&A — and they will not spend executive time on LMS features. They listen for "measurable outcomes," "audit-ready," and "reduce operational burden on L&D."
