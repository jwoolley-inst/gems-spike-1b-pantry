Build a blog post by pulling one messaging pillar as the anchor, one or two pain points that match the audience persona, one or two proof points that back the pillar, and (optionally) a statistic or customer quote. Every sentence runs through the tone_rule filters before it ships. Lead with a specific observation — a teacher name, a district outcome, an enrollment number. The reader should recognize themselves in the first paragraph. Trim anything that reads like a brochure. If the draft needs more than one subhead, split it into two posts.
