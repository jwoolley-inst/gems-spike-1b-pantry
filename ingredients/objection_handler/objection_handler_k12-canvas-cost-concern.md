Frame the cost of the current stack, not Canvas. Counted in full — integration engineering hours, SSO break/fix, grade-sync escalations, teacher training time re-paid every year — the current sprawl is rarely cheaper. Canvas absorbs that friction on day 90 and saves teacher and IT time on day 365 and every day after.
