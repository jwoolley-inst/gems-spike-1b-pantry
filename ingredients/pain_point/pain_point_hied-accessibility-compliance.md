Institutions face rising expectations — and in some cases, OCR complaints — around ADA and Section 508 compliance in digital course materials. Faculty don't have time to build accessible content from scratch; instructional design teams can't scale to hand-audit every course. The compliance risk is institutional even though the authoring is distributed.
