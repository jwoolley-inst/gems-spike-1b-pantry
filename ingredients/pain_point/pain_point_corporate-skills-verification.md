"Completion" is not "capability." Orgs need defensible evidence that a learner can actually do the work — not just that they clicked through the course. The gap between completion and capability shows up in ramp-time, in customer escalations, and in the quiet skills inventory gaps that only surface during an M&A or a reorg.
