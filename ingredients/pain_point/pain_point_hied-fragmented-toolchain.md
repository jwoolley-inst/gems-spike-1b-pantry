Faculty report stitching together Zoom, Canvas, a shared drive, email, and a proctoring tool for a single course — with each term bringing new integration friction. Most of the pain is not any single tool, it's the seams between them. Students feel the fragmentation, too, as they hunt across platforms for the thing due tomorrow.
