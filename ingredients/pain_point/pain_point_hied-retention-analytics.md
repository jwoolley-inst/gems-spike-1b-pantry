Institutions routinely identify at-risk students weeks after the intervention window has closed. Advisors rely on mid-term grade reports and self-reported flags; by then the student has already disengaged. Without timely behavioral signal — logins, assignment submission, resource access — retention programs are reactive, not preventive.
