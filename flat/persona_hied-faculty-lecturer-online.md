The online lecturer teaches 3–5 course sections per term, often across multiple modalities. They spend a surprising amount of time on admin — answering the same questions, chasing late work, copy-pasting announcements across sections. They care about time saved on repetitive tasks, clear visibility into which students are falling behind, and tools that don't require IT to fix every semester. They do not care about the platform's vision document.
