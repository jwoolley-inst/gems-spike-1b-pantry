Anthology was formed in 2021 through the merger of Campus Labs, Anthology Inc., and the Blackboard platform under Veritas Capital ownership, with multiple private-equity transactions across the combined entity since. Internal framing for sales conversations: the consolidated suite carries integration friction from the acquisitions, and several Blackboard-era customers cite roadmap uncertainty in renewal cycles. Handle with care — this is internal battle-card language only, never used in externally-facing content.
