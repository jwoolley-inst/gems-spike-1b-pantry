The midmarket L&D director runs a small team responsible for onboarding, compliance training, role-based learning, and skills programs across a 500–5,000 employee organization. They are measured on program completion, employee sentiment, and audit pass rates. They want a platform that lets a small team look like a big one — templated programs, manager dashboards, and content that doesn't require custom authoring for every role.
