Acknowledge the change-fatigue cost — it's real. Then name the specific tradeoff: one more migration, or keep absorbing recurring friction every semester forever. Districts that phase rollouts per-school with teacher champions hit 92% adoption inside a semester without a teacher revolt. Lead with the rollout plan, not the product tour.
