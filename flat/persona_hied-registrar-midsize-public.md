The Registrar owns transcripts, credentials, enrollment verification, and the systems of record behind them. Their ops team burns hours every week on transcript requests, verifications, and rush processing for graduating students. They measure themselves on turnaround time, data integrity, and audit readiness. They want digital credentialing that integrates with the SIS, doesn't force staff to learn new tools, and reduces manual verification volume.
