Agree the stakes, then shrink them. Migration risk is real; what's shared is the shape of the program that manages it — pilot cohort in a single college, parallel operation across at least one term, course-template library built before faculty touch the system, and an at-scale rehearsal weekend before cutover. Offer three named references who ran the same playbook.
