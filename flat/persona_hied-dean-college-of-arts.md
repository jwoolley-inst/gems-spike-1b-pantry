The Dean is caught between faculty autonomy and institutional standardization. They want consistent student experience across 300 course sections without mandating pedagogy. They care about accreditation evidence, equity of access across course modalities, and junior faculty onboarding time. They will champion a platform that saves faculty hours without feeling like surveillance.
