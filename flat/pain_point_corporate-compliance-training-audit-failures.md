Regulators and internal audit teams increasingly require not just training completion but defensible evidence — who was assigned, when they were trained, on what version of the content, and how the organization verified retention. Legacy LMS exports produce rows that don't survive a probing audit. Findings follow; leadership attention follows the findings.
